About

QE Leader.

Practitioner.

I started as a Mechanical Engineer and taught myself quality engineering — which means I've never taken the discipline for granted. That practitioner's instinct has shaped everything: the frameworks, the tooling, the way I run teams.

Today I lead an AI-native QE Centre of Excellence at Litmus7 Systems Consulting, with P&L ownership and enterprise clients across retail, aviation, and fintech. We don't just test software — we protect revenue.

Alongside that I'm building QEVantage™ — an independent platform that unifies functional, automation, performance, and security testing under a single intelligence layer, culminating in a Release Readiness Score™ that gives engineering leaders a defensible go/no-go signal before every deployment.

I write, build, and ship in public. If you're rethinking what quality engineering means in an AI-native world — let's talk.
